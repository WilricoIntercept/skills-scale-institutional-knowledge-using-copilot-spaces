# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Testing

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality by designing test strategies, executing tests, and validating that features meet acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated tests
- Report and track defects
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Collaborate on testability during feature design

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Ensure features meet quality standards and user expectations
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with Product and Project Managers
- Bug reports and defect triage meetings
- Test results shared in sprint reviews
- Feedback during PR reviews and design discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on testability, reproduce bugs, verify fixes
- **Product Manager**: Validate acceptance criteria, provide quality feedback
- **Project Manager**: Report testing progress and risks
- **Technical Writer**: Validate documentation accuracy through testing

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, coach teams on agile practices, and remove impediments that block delivery progress.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments for the team
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Shield the team from external interruptions
- Promote continuous improvement and team health

### Goals
- Enable team self-organization and high performance
- Ensure adherence to agile practices and ceremonies
- Foster a culture of transparency and continuous improvement

### Typical Communication
- Facilitate all agile ceremonies
- Regular check-ins with team members about impediments
- Coordination with Project Manager on risks and dependencies
- Retrospective action item tracking

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Manager**: Align on scheduling, risks, blockers, and stakeholder communication
- **Product Manager**: Support backlog refinement and prioritization discussions
- **Developers**: Remove impediments, facilitate collaboration
- **QA/Testing**: Ensure quality practices are integrated into sprints

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive user experiences through research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing to ensure products meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and synthesize insights
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and information architecture
- Conduct usability testing and iterate on designs
- Maintain design systems and style guides
- Ensure accessibility standards are met

### Goals
- Create user-centered, accessible, and intuitive interfaces
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Balance user needs with technical and business constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings presentations
- Design handoff documentation
- Feedback sessions with stakeholders and users

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborate on requirements, user needs, and customer insights
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications, clarify implementation details, validate feasibility
- **QA/Testing**: Define usability test cases, validate user experience
- **Business Analyst**: Align design solutions with business requirements
- **Technical Writer**: Ensure UI terminology matches documentation

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical solutions by gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and translating stakeholder needs into specifications.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Create process maps and workflow diagrams
- Analyze business processes for improvement opportunities
- Define functional specifications and user stories
- Support user acceptance testing and validation
- Facilitate requirements workshops with stakeholders

### Goals
- Ensure solutions align with business objectives
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements
- Identify process improvements and efficiency gains

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and specifications
- Stakeholder workshops and interviews
- Business case presentations
- Status updates on requirements changes

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborate on requirements definition, product vision, and feature prioritization
- **Project Manager**: Communicate scope changes, timeline impacts, and dependencies
- **Developers**: Clarify requirements, answer questions, support estimation
- **QA/Testing**: Define acceptance criteria, participate in UAT
- **UX Designer**: Ensure requirements support user experience goals

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate documentation including user guides, API documentation, release notes, and internal technical documentation.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user manuals and help documentation
- Create API documentation and integration guides
- Produce release notes and change logs
- Document technical processes and architecture
- Ensure documentation accuracy and clarity
- Manage documentation review and approval workflows

### Goals
- Enable users and developers to successfully use products
- Reduce support burden through clear documentation
- Maintain up-to-date and accessible documentation

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with SMEs
- Release notes coordination
- Feedback collection from users and support teams
- Style guide and documentation standards discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Gather technical details, review feature documentation, validate accuracy
- **QA/Testing**: Validate documented procedures, test documentation steps
- **Product Manager**: Understand feature intent and user needs
- **UX Designer**: Align terminology and UI descriptions
- **Project Manager**: Coordinate documentation deadlines with release schedules
